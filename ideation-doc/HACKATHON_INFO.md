# Build with TRAE x MiniMax Hackathon

**24-hour AI hackathon at USC | March 27-28, 2026**

---

## Theme

**AI Agents for Productivity & Life Hacks**

Build agents that automate or simplify daily tasks. Use TRAE AI as your development environment and integrate MiniMax APIs to power your agent.

### Bonus Track: Photon Integration
Deploy your agents to iMessage using Photon's messaging platform.

### Example Ideas
- **Study Assistant** — organizes notes, generates flashcards, creates study schedules based on upcoming exams
- **Email Triage System** — automatically categorize, summarize, and draft replies for incoming emails
- **Meal Planner** — generate weekly meal plans based on dietary preferences, budget, and fridge contents
- **Finance Tracker** — monitors spending, categorizes transactions, provides actionable savings tips

## Rules

1. **Team Size** — 2-5 members per team. At least one member must write code.
2. **Use TRAE AI** — TRAE AI must be your primary development environment throughout the hackathon.
3. **MiniMax API Integration** — Integrate at least one MiniMax API into your project (text, vision, speech, or video).
4. **Start Fresh** — Start your project during the 24-hour hackathon window. No pre-built projects allowed.
5. **Physical Check-In** — At least 75% of your team must physically check in by 11:00 AM PT on March 28.
6. **No Check-In = No Prizes** — Teams that do not complete physical check-in are ineligible for prizes.
